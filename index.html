Who are we?

Welcome to Jake's Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a story and every sip is an experience.

Located in the vibrant heart of Manila, Jake's is more than just a café; it's a cozy escape from the bustling city streets. Our obsession with coffee drives us to source the finest beans, ensuring each brew is perfect.

But Jake's is more than just coffee. We're a community hub, hosting live music nights and art exhibitions. With free Wi-Fi and cozy corners, we invite you to stay awhile and make yourself at home.

Join us at Jake's Coffee Shop for great coffee and unforgettable moments. Welcome to your new favorite spot in Manila.
